# Video Pitch Studio - User Guide

## 📖 **For Students: How to Create Your Video Pitch**

Welcome to the Video Pitch Studio! This guide will walk you through creating a professional video pitch in just 5-10 minutes.

---

## 🎯 **Why Create a Video Pitch?**

A video pitch helps you:
- ✅ **Stand out** from other candidates
- ✅ **Showcase personality** beyond your CV
- ✅ **Demonstrate communication skills**
- ✅ **Make a memorable first impression**
- ✅ **Share easily** on LinkedIn, CV, or email

---

## 🚀 **Quick Start (5 Minutes)**

### **Step 1: Access the Studio**

**Option A: From Dashboard**
1. Login to PlacementsPortal
2. Go to your Student Dashboard
3. Click the **"Video Pitch"** tab (has a NEW badge)
4. Click **"Create Your Pitch Now"**

**Option B: From Navbar**
1. Login to PlacementsPortal
2. Click your profile icon (top right)
3. Select **"Video Pitch Studio"** from the dropdown
4. The studio will open

**Direct URL:** `http://localhost:3000/studio`

---

## 🎬 **The 3-Step Process**

### **STEP 1: Generate Your Script** ✍️

**Option A: AI-Generated (Recommended)**
1. Click **"Generate My Pitch"** button
2. Wait 5-10 seconds while AI creates your script
3. Your personalized 45-second pitch will appear

**Option B: Write Your Own**
1. Type your script in the text box
2. Aim for ~110 words (45 seconds when spoken)
3. Keep it professional but personal

**Tips for a Great Script:**
- Start with a brief introduction
- Highlight 2-3 key skills or achievements
- Mention what you're looking for
- End with a call-to-action
- Keep it under 60 seconds

**Edit Your Script:**
- Click in the textarea to edit
- Word count updates in real-time
- Est. duration shown below

**When Ready:**
- Click **"Proceed to Recording"**

---

### **STEP 2: Record Your Video** 🎥

**First Time Setup:**
1. Browser will ask for **Camera** and **Microphone** permissions
2. Click **"Allow"** to grant access
3. Your webcam preview will appear

**Before Recording:**

**A. Adjust Settings** (click settings icon)
- **Scroll Speed:** Adjust how fast the script scrolls (20-60 WPM)
  - Slower = More time to read
  - Faster = Less time per line
  - Default: 30 WPM (good for most people)
  
- **Mirror Video:** Toggle on/off
  - ON = Mirrored (like looking in a mirror)
  - OFF = Normal (how others see you)
  
- **Show/Hide Teleprompter:** Click eye icon
  - ON = Script visible while recording
  - OFF = No script overlay (for experienced speakers)

**B. Position Yourself**
- Center your face in the frame
- Good lighting (face the window/light)
- Clean background
- Look at the camera (not the screen)

**Start Recording:**
1. Click **"Start Recording"** (big red button)
2. Count "3, 2, 1" in your head
3. Start speaking naturally
4. The script will **auto-scroll** as you speak
5. Try to keep pace with the scrolling text
6. Recording stops automatically at 60 seconds

**During Recording:**
- 🔴 **LIVE indicator** shows you're recording
- **Timer** counts up (MM:SS)
- **Progress bar** fills from 0-60 seconds
- Script **scrolls automatically** at your set speed

**Stop Recording:**
- Click **"Stop Recording"** (red button)
- Or wait for auto-stop at 60 seconds

**Review Your Video:**
- Video plays back automatically
- Watch and decide if you're happy with it

**Options:**
- ✅ **Keep it:** Click **"Save & Upload"**
- 🔄 **Re-record:** Click **"Re-record"** to try again
- ⬇️ **Download:** Save locally before uploading

---

**After Uploading:**

## ⚙️ **Troubleshooting**

### **Camera/Microphone Issues**

**Problem:** "Unable to access camera"
- **Solution:** 
  1. Check browser permissions
  2. Allow camera access
  3. Refresh page
  4. Try different browser (Chrome recommended)

**Problem:** Camera shows but no audio
- **Solution:**
  1. Check microphone permissions
  2. Select correct microphone in browser settings
  3. Test microphone in system settings

**Problem:** Camera shows someone else or wrong camera
- **Solution:**
  1. Close other apps using camera (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
  2. Browser will use default camera
  3. Change default in system settings

### **Recording Issues**

**Problem:** Recording button doesn't work
- **Solution:**
  1. Ensure camera permission granted
  2. Refresh page
  3. Clear browser cache
  4. Try incognito/private mode

**Problem:** Script doesn't scroll
- **Solution:**
  1. Check "Show Script" is enabled (eye icon)
  2. Adjust scroll speed in settings
  3. Recording must be started for auto-scroll

**Problem:** Video stops before I finish
- **Solution:**
  - 60-second limit is enforced
  - Edit your script to be shorter
  - Speak slightly faster

### **Upload Issues**

**Problem:** "Failed to save video pitch"
- **Solution:**
  1. Check internet connection
  2. Video might be too large
  3. Try recording again (shorter)
  4. Download locally as backup

**Problem:** Link doesn't work
- **Solution:**
  1. Copy the full URL
  2. Include https:// if sharing
  3. Test in incognito mode first

## 🎓 **Best Practices**

### **Script Length:**
- **45 seconds (ideal)** = ~110 words
- **60 seconds (max)** = ~150 words
- Speak at normal pace (not too fast/slow)

### **Visual Presentation:**
- **Dress:** Business casual or smart casual
- **Background:** Clean, uncluttered, professional
- **Lighting:** Natural light or lamp in front of you
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders visible, centered

### **Content:**
- **Be specific:** Not "I'm good at programming" → "I built 5 React apps"
- **Be authentic:** Let your personality shine
- **Be concise:** Every word counts
- **Be positive:** Focus on what you can do, not can't

### **Delivery:**
- **Energy:** Match the role (enthusiastic for marketing, calm for finance)
- **Pace:** Not too fast (nervous) or too slow (boring)
- **Tone:** Professional but approachable
- **Body language:** Open, confident, friendly

## 🔒 **Privacy & Security**

### **What's Stored:**
- Your video file (secure cloud storage)
- Public link (shareable, not indexed by Google)
- Script text (for your reference)
- View count (how many people watched)

### **What's Not Stored:**
- Your camera stream is NOT recorded until you click "Start"
- We don't access your camera without permission
- Videos are private unless you share the link

### **Deleting Your Pitch:**
- Re-record any time (replaces old video)
- Contact support to completely remove

## 💬 **FAQs**

**Q: How long does it take?**
A: 5-10 minutes total (2 min script, 3 min recording, 5 min setup)

**Q: Can I re-record?**
A: Yes! Unlimited re-recordings. Previous video is replaced.

**Q: Do I need AI for the script?**
A: No, you can write your own. AI is optional but helpful.

**Q: What if I don't like my video?**
A: Click "Re-record" and try again. No limit.

**Q: Can recruiters see who watched?**
A: No, only you see view counts.

**Q: Is my video public?**
A: Only people with your link can watch. Not searchable.

**Q: Can I edit the video after recording?**
A: No editing tools yet. Best to re-record if needed.

**Q: What video format is used?**
A: WebM (VP9 codec) - plays in all modern browsers

**Q: How big is the video file?**
A: Typically 5-15MB for 60 seconds

**Q: Can I download my video?**
A: Yes! Click "Download" before or after uploading
